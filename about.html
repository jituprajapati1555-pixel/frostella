Inside the Churn

How We Craft Magic

Our rigorous 4-step churning process ensures unmatched density and scoopability.

🐄

01. Milk Pasteurization

Raw milk from our partner farms is heated mildly to lock in fresh flavors without depleting crucial enzymes.

⏳

02. The Aging Lock

The milk base is aged at 4°C for 24 hours, letting milk fat crystals fully bind for a velvety texture.

🌀

03. Slow Micro-Churning

We churn slowly in batch freezers to limit overrun (trapped air), creating ultra-dense, flavorful ice cream.

❄️

04. Blast Hardening

Within 2 minutes of churning, tubs are shock-frozen at -35°C, preventing large ice crystals from forming.
