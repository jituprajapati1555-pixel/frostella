Ingredient Focus — Part 01

Single-Origin Organic Milk

We partner with local dairy farms that prioritize animal welfare and organic pastures. No hormones, no preservatives—just rich, natural cream churned to perfection.

Ingredient Focus — Part 02

Madagascar Bourbon Vanilla & Fine Cocoa

We travel the globe to import the highest grade ingredients. Real Bourbon vanilla beans hand-seeded in Madagascar, and Venezuelan single-estate cacao beans ground locally.

Ingredient Focus — Part 03

Hand-Crafted in Small Batches

To maintain premium density and creamy texture, our gelatieri prepare every flavour in micro-batches under 50 liters. It is a slow, loving process you can taste in every single spoonful.
